wp_static

A frozen weighted predictor.

A frozen take on the JPEG XL self-correcting weighted predictor. The original retunes its weights on every sample from recent errors. Freezing them leaves a fixed linear function of five causal neighbours, W plus a kernel K over N, NW, NE and NN. W enters with weight one, so P = W + K and a row still decodes as a prefix sum. The four weights and a right shift are trained per tile and ride in the header, and the defaults turn it back into planar.
