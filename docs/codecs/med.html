med

The median edge detector.

The predictor from JPEG-LS. It takes the median of W, N and the planar gradient W+N−NW, which is the same as clamping that gradient between W and N. On smooth ground the gradient passes through untouched. At an edge it overshoots and the clamp catches it, landing on N for a vertical edge or W for a horizontal one. The choice depends on W nonlinearly, so samples decode one at a time.
