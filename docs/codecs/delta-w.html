delta_w

Each value, minus its west neighbour.

The simplest spatial predictor. Every sample is stored as the difference from the one to its left; the first sample of a row is kept whole as a seed. The carry reseeds at every row edge, so rows never affect each other. On smooth horizontal gradients the residuals collapse toward zero, which is exactly what the downstream entropy stage compresses best.
